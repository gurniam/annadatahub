📋 How to Apply for PMFBY
1
Visit nearest bank, CSC or pmfby.gov.in
2
Bring: Aadhaar, Land records (Khasra/Khatauni), Bank details, Sowing certificate
3
Fill PMFBY form — deadline is last date of sowing month
4
Pay premium — bank deducts automatically if you have KCC
5
If crop fails — file claim within 72 hours of loss
6
Money credited to bank within 15-20 days of approval
✅ What PMFBY Covers
✅Natural disasters (flood, drought, cyclone, hailstorm)
✅Pest and disease attacks on standing crop
✅Post-harvest losses (within 14 days of harvest)
✅Prevented sowing due to drought
❌War, nuclear risks, theft
❌Intentional damage by farmer
