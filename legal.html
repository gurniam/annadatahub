⚠️ Disclaimer
AI-Generated Information

AnnadataHub uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to generate farming advice including crop disease analysis, fertilizer recommendations, mandi price estimates, weather farming advice, crop calendars and agricultural news.

This AI-generated information:

  • Is for general awareness and guidance only — not professional advice
  • May not be accurate for your specific field, soil or local conditions
  • Must be verified with your local Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or agricultural officer before acting
  • Should not be the sole basis for major farming or financial decisions
  • Accuracy may vary — AI systems can make mistakes
Crop Disease Scanner

The AI crop disease scanner analyzes photos to suggest possible diseases. This is an assistive tool — not a certified agricultural diagnosis.

  • Results depend heavily on photo quality, lighting and angle
  • Always confirm with a trained agricultural scientist or KVK officer
  • Do not apply pesticides or chemicals based solely on AI diagnosis
  • Wrong diagnosis could damage your crop — always verify first
  • For urgent crop disease, call Kisan Helpline: 1800-180-1551 (Free)
Mandi Prices

Mandi prices shown are AI estimates based on historical data. These are NOT live prices from APMC mandis or AGMARKNET.

  • Actual prices may be significantly different from estimates shown
  • Always verify at your local mandi before selling your crop
  • For official live prices visit: agmarknet.gov.in (Government of India)
  • Do not take financial decisions based on our price estimates alone
Government Schemes Information

Scheme information (PM-KISAN, PMFBY, KCC, PM-KUSUM etc.) is provided for awareness only.

  • Scheme terms, eligibility and amounts may change — verify at official websites
  • AnnadataHub is NOT affiliated with any government department or ministry
  • We are a private digital service — not a government portal
  • For official scheme information always visit official government websites
